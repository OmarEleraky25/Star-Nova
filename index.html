Introduction

01 / 07

Purpose

This SMS Manual outlines the framework, policies, and processes for ensuring a safe and secure environment for all operations related to Star Nova. Designed to meet ICAO, local authority, and industry best practices.

Scope

  • Flight Operations
  • Maintenance Standards
  • Ground Operations

Executive Commitment

"Top Management is committed to providing adequate resources and support to ensure the safety management system is implemented effectively across all operational sectors."
